Privacy Policy

Last Updated: March 8, 2025

This Privacy Policy describes how My Look ("we", "us", or "our") collects, uses, and discloses your information when you use our mobile application (the "Service").

1. Information We Collect

a. Personal Information

When you create an account, we collect your email address and authentication credentials via our third-party provider, Supabase.

b. User Content (Images)

Our Service allows you to upload or capture photos of outfits for AI analysis. These images are processed temporarily to provide styling feedback. We do not use your photos to train public AI models without your explicit consent.

c. Usage Data

We may collect information on how the Service is accessed and used, including device type, operating system, and interaction logs.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your data to:

  • Provide and maintain the Service.
  • Authenticate your identity.
  • Process images using Google Gemini AI to generate styling advice.
  • Improve app performance and fix bugs.

3. Third-Party Services

We utilize the following third-party services which may process your data according to their own privacy policies:

  • Supabase: For authentication and database services.
  • Google Gemini AI: For image analysis and text generation.
  • Expo: For app infrastructure and updates.

4. Data Retention and Deletion

We retain your personal information only for as long as is necessary for the purposes set out in this Privacy Policy. You can request the deletion of your account and associated data at any time by visiting our Deletion Request Page.

5. Children's Privacy

Our Service does not address anyone under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personally identifiable information from children under 13.

6. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page.
