We respect your privacy. If you wish to delete your account and all associated data, please follow the steps below.

Step 1: Initiate Request

Click the button below to send us an official deletion request via email. Please ensure you send this from the email address associated with your account.

Send Deletion Request

Step 2: Processing

Our team will verify your request. Once verified, we will initiate the deletion process.

Data Deletion Policy

Upon processing your request, the following data will be permanently deleted:

  • Your User Profile (Email, Name, ID)
  • All Authentication Records
  • Any saved preferences or settings
  • Any uploaded images or analysis history

Retention Period: We process all deletion requests within 30 days. No data is retained after this period, except where required by law.
